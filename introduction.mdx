## What is MenuBarText?

MenuBarText is a lightweight macOS app that displays customizable text in your menu bar area when it's hidden. Keep your motivation, reminders, or important notes always visible while you work.

Designed for developers, students, and professionals who want persistent text reminders without the distraction of full applications or notifications.

## How it works

MenuBarText consists of two lightweight components:

1. **MenuBarTextOverlay.app** - Displays the actual text as a transparent overlay
2. **MenuBarTextController.app** - Provides menu bar controls and settings

Both apps communicate through file-based updates for real-time changes without restart.

<Note>
  MenuBarText works seamlessly with macOS auto-hide menu bar. The text appears when your menu bar is hidden and temporarily disappears when the menu bar is shown.
</Note>

## Requirements

- macOS 13.0 or later
- Menu bar set to auto-hide (System Settings → Desktop & Dock → Automatically hide and show the menu bar)
